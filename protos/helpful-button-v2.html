Design brief — which fits DishCheck best?

1 · Segmented
Clear, professional. Familiar from GitHub. Two info zones in one widget.
2 · Burst ★
Most delightful. Brand colours in the burst. Memorable. Works great for a food app.
3 · Sweep
Warm, branded, smooth. Unique to DishCheck. Subtle and satisfying.
4 · Stacked
Minimal footprint. Good when space is tight. PH pattern is well-understood.
5 · Float +1
Playful reward feeling. Almost no chrome — disappears into the meta row.
6 · Badge
Clean at rest. Badge pop is a nice reveal. Count feels like social proof.
