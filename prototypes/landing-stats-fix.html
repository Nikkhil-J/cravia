⚠️ What's wrong with the numbers

Your getDishCount() returns all dish documents in Firestore (menu items), not dishes that have reviews. Showing "131+ Dishes reviewed" next to "2 Restaurants" creates a credibility mismatch — readers think "there's no way 131 unique dishes were reviewed at only 2 restaurants." The number is technically right (you catalogued 131 menu items), but the label "reviewed" is misleading. Below are three redesign options, each solving this differently.
